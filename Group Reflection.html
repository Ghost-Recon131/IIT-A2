Group Reflection
Individual
Daniel
Throughout the project, the group was able to stay on track with their workload, progressing each section in a moderate pace. When issues would arrive whether from being sick or facing lots of work in other courses, we were able to help each other out by working on parts together to ease the workload. Although we could handle the work evenly, there would however be issues with communication, having inconsistent moments of being able to talk together as a full team to discuss progress. If I were to list something surprising, it would be the speed of the output of some of the sections involved. In some cases, when asked to make progress towards completing one part of the report, some members were able to finish in the same night, if not a day or two later. Being in a group has taught me how much progress can be made with the assistance of others, as I often focused on working alone previously. Being my first semester, I have learned to carefully consider the members in my group and how I work with them if given the opportunity.
Phoebe
One thing I found worked well within our group was the ability to delegate tasks early on. Our team had assigned tasks within the first meeting, which personally worked well for me as I knew what I was doing from the get-go meaning I could get started ASAP. One thing I believe could be worked upon is collaboration within the team. Rather than assigning a separate task to each team member I believe in future it would be more beneficial to work through tasks together. If we were able to get everyone’s input not only, would it relieve everyone’s workload, but it would also result in a more cohesive result. One thing I found extremely surprising is that everyone worked on their parts, and no one had to be constantly chased up for part submission, which is something I have begun to expect with group work. It was pleasant to have a team where everyone contributed fairly. During this assignment I found that working within a group can be very enriching when you have team members who are contributing fairly, as mentioned above I have never had a group where everyone was doing their part, so again this was a nice change.
Praniv
Our group started off well as we allocated all the tasks early one and had some days and times set for our recordings. However even though we had a fast and efficient start, it sort of slowed down and we didn’t fully stick to our meeting times and days which hindered our group a bit. We had some sections as individual work and we had partners set up for some of the big parts of the project. We also received a new member a towards the later stage of the project which was actually very beneficial as they added a lot of value to our group. One thing to improve upon would be to keep our pace throughout the project and not slow down like we did, although there were some external factors which sometimes caused the slowdown. Some of our original plans did change but the group put in a solid effort to get some work done but sometimes it was hard to get the input and ideas of other members as some didn’t input much, however they still did their parts well. Everyone was willing to put in effort in this group to complete the assignment and when constructive criticism was given members would take it gracefully and improve upon their work instead of taking feedback in a badway.
Jingxuan
Our group had a good plan of dividing all the work early, then work individually or with a partner on our assigned sections. This helped everyone stay on track and reduce instances of depending on another team member to finish a section before more work can be done. Our meetings also went well, with most of the meetings being active and to the point. Everyone also consistency attended. Communication could be further improved, sometimes there are periods where the group is a bit inactive, before picking up again. This leads to the next improving, time management. We started off this assignment well bit then progress slowed down towards the halfway mark before we picked up speed in the last week of submission. One thing that was surprising was how quickly certain tasks were completed, such as the Website Design and structure. Drawing diagrams for the Project Idea section took a bit longer than expected, around 20 minutes per diagram. One thing I learnt about group is that everyone needs to work together for the tasks to be completed. Just completing your own tasks and then ignore other sections will not work out.
Danny
Once the groups had been decided, we quickly allocated who would be doing which sections of the assignment. By doing this we broke up the assignment into smaller chunks of the whole task so that everyone could work and concentrate on their own parts at the same time. What we struggled with was our communication halfway into the assignment due to a combination of mid-semester break disrupting how often we could meet, and everyone being preoccupied with other things. This drastically slowed down our progress with the assignment which showed that our time management and communication is what we should look to improve moving into the next assignment. What was surprising was how quickly we were able to complete our sections once we had started to talk to each other more in the later stages. Overall what I have learnt while working as a group is how vital having good communication and time management is when working with your peers and is hopefully something we can improve on in the next task.
Yuxuan
