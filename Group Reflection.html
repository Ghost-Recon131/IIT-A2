Jingxuan
Our group had a good plan of dividing all the work early, then work individually or with a partner on our assigned sections. This helped everyone stay on track and reduce instances of depending on another team member to finish a section before more work can be done. Our meetings also went well, with most of the meetings being active and to the point. Everyone also consistency attended. Communication could be further improved, sometimes there are periods where the group is a bit inactive, before picking up again. This leads to the next improving, time management. We started off this assignment well bit then progress slowed down towards the halfway mark before we picked up speed in the last week of submission. One thing that was surprising was how quickly certain tasks were completed, such as the Website Design and structure. Drawing diagrams for the Project Idea section took a bit longer than expected, around 20 minutes per diagram. One thing I learnt about group is that everyone needs to work together for the tasks to be completed. Just completing your own tasks and then ignore other sections will not work out.
Danny
Once the groups had been decided, we quickly allocated who would be doing which sections of the assignment. By doing this we broke up the assignment into smaller chunks of the whole task so that everyone could work and concentrate on their own parts at the same time. What we struggled with was our communication halfway into the assignment due to a combination of mid-semester break disrupting how often we could meet, and everyone being preoccupied with other things. This drastically slowed down our progress with the assignment which showed that our time management and communication is what we should look to improve moving into the next assignment. What was surprising was how quickly we were able to complete our sections once we had started to talk to each other more in the later stages. Overall what I have learnt while working as a group is how vital having good communication and time management is when working with your peers and is hopefully something we can improve on in the next task.
