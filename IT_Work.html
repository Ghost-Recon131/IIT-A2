Interview Questions
How did you get into the field of IT?
Didn’t study it, civil engineering (Philippians) ~1yr. Didn’t enjoy as much, different construction sites. Eventually ~early 2000, start of internet boom, lot of opportunity to get trained in IT. Got employed with company that provides training and certification, outsourced in USA. AT&T like telco, supporting US. Started at level 1 IT support, eventually moved up. Got more certification and training. Then moved to Singapore, as IT is in demand, got a good position. Progressed in career. Moved to Australia via skilled migrants as IT was also in demand.
